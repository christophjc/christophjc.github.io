My Week In Peru

Day 1:
    Arrive in Lima, Peru
    Check in to Hotel
    Spend afternoon at Arcomar
    Dinner at Central
Day 2:
    Breakfast at the hotel
    Mario Testino Museum. This is a small and quick museum.
    Spend the Day walking around Malecon(boardwalk) Miraflores, Puente de los Suspiros, and Barranco
    Lunch at Osaka (totally worth the splurge)
    Guided tour around Centro Historico
Day 3:
    Takeoff to Cusco and take a Cab to Ayantatambo
    Stop on the way to Chinchero, Maras, Moray, and Urubamba. Stop for lunch at Tawa Restaurant in Urubamba
    Take the afternoon train to Agua Calientes
    Check into the hotel and have dinner in town
Day 4:
    Early bus to Machu Pichu. Take guided tour throughout the Morning. Make sure to book ahead of time to get the earliest bus available. This will avoid huge crowds and any delays. Be Punctual because if you miss your bus you will not be able to schedule another.
    Take bus down to Agua Calientes and have lunch at XYZ Restaurant
    Take train from Agua Calientes to Cusco. Check in to Hotel Antigua Casona de San Blas
    Dinner at Falling Angel (Also worth the splurge)
Day 5:
    Visit Plaza de Armas, La Catedral, Iglesia de la Compania de Jesus
    Lunch at Limo
    Visit Mercado de San Pedro and Museo de Arte Precolombino
Day 6:
    Visit Sacsayhuman, Quirikancha, Convento de Santo Domingo, San Blas, and Cena de Chicha
    Have dinner at Chicha
Day 7:
    Bus to Cruz del Sur. Stop to Puno Check in to Hotel Libertador. Beautiful private island where you can walk around, take in the scenery and find a charming restaurant for dinner and drinks.
Day 8:
    Visit Islas de los Urus and Isla Taquile
Day 9:
    Travel back to Lima and have a relaxing Evening
Day 10:
    Travel back home
